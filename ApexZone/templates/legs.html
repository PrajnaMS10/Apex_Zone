Legs Exercises

Squats

ID: 1

Muscles Worked: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, lower back, calves.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Keep your chest up, shoulders back, and core engaged.
  3. Lower your body by bending your knees and hips as if sitting back into a chair.
  4. Go down until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
  5. Push through your heels to return to the starting position.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Keep your knees in line with your toes.
  • Avoid letting your knees cave inward or move forward past your toes.
  • Maintain a neutral spine throughout the movement.

Leg Press

ID: 2

Muscles Worked: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes.

Instructions:

  1. Sit on the leg press machine and place your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform.
  2. Adjust the seat and backrest if necessary to ensure proper alignment.
  3. Press the platform away to release the safety latches.
  4. Lower the platform by bending your knees until they are at a 90-degree angle.
  5. Push the platform back up to the starting position without locking your knees.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Keep your feet flat on the platform and your back against the seat.
  • Avoid lifting your heels or letting your knees collapse inward.

Romanian Deadlifts

ID: 3

Muscles Worked: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart and hold a barbell with an overhand grip.
  2. Keep your knees slightly bent and your back straight.
  3. Hinge at your hips, pushing them back as you lower the barbell along the front of your legs.
  4. Lower the barbell until you feel a stretch in your hamstrings, typically just below the knees.
  5. Return to the starting position by driving your hips forward and standing up straight.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Maintain a neutral spine throughout the movement.
  • Avoid rounding your back.
  • Keep the barbell close to your body.

Leg Curls

ID: 4

Muscles Worked: Hamstrings.

Instructions:

  1. Lie face down on the leg curl machine with your legs fully extended and the pad just above your ankles.
  2. Grab the handles for support and keep your hips down.
  3. Curl your legs up as far as possible by bending your knees.
  4. Slowly lower the weight back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Use a controlled movement to avoid momentum.
  • Focus on squeezing your hamstrings at the top of the movement.

Calf Raises

ID: 5

Muscles Worked: Calves (gastrocnemius and soleus).

Instructions:

  1. Stand with the balls of your feet on the edge of a step or a raised platform.
  2. Lower your heels below the step to feel a stretch in your calves.
  3. Push through the balls of your feet to raise your heels as high as possible.
  4. Slowly lower back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Keep your knees straight but not locked.
  • Use a full range of motion to maximize effectiveness.
  • Maintain a steady pace without bouncing.
