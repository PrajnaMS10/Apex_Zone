Chest and Arms Exercises

Bench Press

ID: 6

Muscles Worked: Pectorals, deltoids, triceps.

Instructions:

  1. Lie flat on a bench with your feet flat on the ground.
  2. Grip the barbell with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  3. Unrack the bar and lower it slowly to your chest, just below the nipples.
  4. Push the bar back up until your arms are fully extended.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Keep your back flat and maintain a natural arch.
  • Do not bounce the bar off your chest.

Incline Dumbbell Press

ID: 7

Muscles Worked: Upper pectorals, deltoids, triceps.

Instructions:

  1. Set an incline bench to about 30-45 degrees.
  2. Sit on the bench and hold a dumbbell in each hand, resting on your thighs.
  3. Lie back and press the dumbbells above your chest with your palms facing forward.
  4. Lower the dumbbells slowly to the sides of your chest.
  5. Press the dumbbells back up to the starting position.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Keep your elbows slightly bent to avoid overextending your shoulder joints.
  • Focus on a controlled movement, avoiding momentum.

Chest Flyes

ID: 8

Muscles Worked: Pectorals, deltoids.

Instructions:

  1. Lie flat on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing each other.
  2. Extend your arms above your chest, elbows slightly bent.
  3. Lower the dumbbells out to the sides in an arc, feeling the stretch in your chest.
  4. Bring the dumbbells back up to the starting position, squeezing your chest muscles.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Keep a slight bend in your elbows throughout the movement.
  • Focus on using your chest muscles to move the weights, not your arms.

Push-Ups or Dips

ID: 9

Muscles Worked: Pectorals, deltoids, triceps.

Push-Ups Instructions:

  1. Start in a plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor.
  3. Push yourself back up to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Dips Instructions:

  1. Position yourself between parallel bars and lift your body so your arms are straight.
  2. Lower your body by bending your elbows until your shoulders are below your elbows.
  3. Push yourself back up to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • For push-ups, keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • For dips, avoid swinging your body; use controlled movements.

Barbell Bicep Curls

ID: 10

Muscles Worked: Biceps.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell with an underhand grip (palms facing up).
  2. Keep your elbows close to your torso and curl the barbell up to shoulder level.
  3. Slowly lower the barbell back to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Avoid swinging your body; use only your arms.
  • Keep your elbows stationary and close to your body.

Tricep Dips or Tricep Pushdowns

ID: 11

Muscles Worked: Triceps.

Tricep Dips Instructions:

  1. Position yourself between parallel bars and lift your body so your arms are straight.
  2. Lower your body by bending your elbows until your shoulders are below your elbows.
  3. Push yourself back up to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tricep Pushdowns Instructions:

  1. Attach a rope or bar to a high pulley on a cable machine.
  2. Stand facing the machine and grab the attachment with an overhand grip.
  3. Keep your elbows close to your torso and push the attachment down until your arms are fully extended.
  4. Slowly return to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • For dips, keep your body upright to target the triceps more effectively.
  • For pushdowns, avoid flaring your elbows out.
