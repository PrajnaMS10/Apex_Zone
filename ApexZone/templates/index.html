BMI

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator works by using a person's weight and height to determine their body mass index, which is a measure of body fat. By inputting these values, the calculator provides a BMI number, which categorizes the individual into underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.This simple tool helps assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.
