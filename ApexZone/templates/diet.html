DIETPLAN

CARBOHYDRATES

Whole Grains: Brown rice, whole grain bread, whole wheat pasta , barley .

Legumes: Lentils, chickpeas, black beans, and kidney beans.

Vegetables: Sweet potatoes, carrots, beets, and winter squash.

Fruits: Bananas, apples, berries, and pears.

Dairy: Yogurt and milk (also provide protein and healthy fats).

FATS

Avocados: Rich in monounsaturated fats, vitamins, and minerals.

Nuts and Seeds: Almonds, walnuts, chia seeds, flaxseeds, and sunflower seeds provide healthy fats, protein, and fiber.

Nut Butters: Peanut butter, almond butter, and other nut butters are calorie-dense and nutrient-rich.

Oils: Olive oil, coconut oil, avocado oil, and flaxseed oil are great for cooking and adding to dishes.

Fatty Fish: Salmon, mackerel, sardines, and trout are high in omega-3 fatty acids.

Dairy Products: Full-fat yogurt, cheese, and milk provide both healthy fats and protein.

Dark Chocolate: Contains healthy fats and antioxidants.

Eggs: A good source of both protein and fat.

Others

FIBERS:Whole grains, fruits, vegetables, legumes, nuts, seeds.

Probiotics and Prebiotics: Yogurt, kefir, sauerkraut, kimchi (probiotics); garlic, onions, bananas, asparagus (prebiotics).

Antioxidants:Berries, dark chocolate, pecans, kidney beans.

PROTEIN

Lean Meats: Chicken, turkey, lean beef, and pork.

Fish and Seafood: Salmon, tuna, mackerel, shrimp, and other seafood.

Eggs: Whole eggs and egg whites.

Dairy Products: Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, milk, and cheese.

Legumes: Lentils, chickpeas, black beans, and kidney beans.

Nuts and Seeds: Almonds, peanuts, chia seeds, and flaxseeds.

Soy Products: Tofu, tempeh, and edamame.

Protein Supplements: Whey protein, casein protein, and plant-based protein powders.

MINERALS

Calcium: Crucial for bone health.

Sources: Dairy products, fortified plant milks, leafy greens, tofu.

Iron: Important for oxygen transport and energy levels.

Sources: Red meat, poultry, fish, lentils, beans, spinach, fortified cereals.

Magnesium: Supports muscle and nerve function, energy production.

Sources: Nuts, seeds, whole grains, leafy greens.

Zinc: Necessary for immune function and wound healing.

Sources: Meat, shellfish, legumes, seeds, nuts.

WATER

2.7 to 3.7 liters per day
