Back and Glutes Exercises

Deadlifts

ID: 12

Muscles Worked: Lower back, glutes, hamstrings, quads, traps.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart, toes under the barbell.
  2. Bend at your hips and knees to grip the barbell with your hands shoulder-width apart.
  3. Keep your back straight and engage your core.
  4. Push through your heels to lift the barbell off the ground, extending your hips and knees.
  5. Stand up straight with the barbell in front of your thighs.
  6. Lower the barbell back to the ground in a controlled manner.
  7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Keep the barbell close to your body throughout the lift.
  • Avoid rounding your back.
  • Maintain a neutral spine and engage your core.

Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldowns

ID: 13

Muscles Worked: Latissimus dorsi, biceps, traps, rhomboids.

Pull-Ups Instructions:

  1. Grab a pull-up bar with an overhand grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Hang with your arms fully extended.
  3. Pull your body up until your chin is above the bar.
  4. Lower yourself back to the starting position in a controlled manner.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Lat Pulldowns Instructions:

  1. Sit at a lat pulldown machine and adjust the thigh pad to secure your legs.
  2. Grab the bar with a wide overhand grip.
  3. Pull the bar down towards your upper chest while squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  4. Slowly return the bar to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • For pull-ups, avoid swinging or using momentum.
  • For lat pulldowns, keep your torso upright and avoid leaning back excessively.

Bent Over Rows

ID: 14

Muscles Worked: Middle back, lats, traps, biceps.

Instructions:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell with an overhand grip.
  2. Bend at your hips and knees, keeping your back straight and torso almost parallel to the floor.
  3. Let the barbell hang at arm's length.
  4. Pull the barbell towards your lower chest or upper abdomen.
  5. Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top of the movement.
  6. Lower the barbell back to the starting position.
  7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Maintain a neutral spine throughout the movement.
  • Avoid using your arms to lift; focus on pulling with your back muscles.

Seated Rows

ID: 15

Muscles Worked: Middle back, lats, traps, biceps.

Instructions:

  1. Sit at a seated row machine and place your feet on the platform.
  2. Grab the handle with an overhand or neutral grip.
  3. Sit upright with your back straight and core engaged.
  4. Pull the handle towards your lower chest, squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  5. Slowly return to the starting position.
  6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Avoid leaning back or using momentum.
  • Keep your chest up and shoulders back.

Face Pulls

ID: 16

Muscles Worked: Rear deltoids, traps, rhomboids.

Instructions:

  1. Attach a rope handle to a high pulley on a cable machine.
  2. Grab the ends of the rope with an overhand grip, palms facing each other.
  3. Step back to create tension in the cable, and stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  4. Pull the rope towards your face, keeping your elbows high and flaring them out.
  5. Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the end of the movement.
  6. Slowly return to the starting position.
  7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

  • Maintain a neutral spine and avoid arching your back.
  • Focus on the contraction of your rear delts and upper back muscles.
